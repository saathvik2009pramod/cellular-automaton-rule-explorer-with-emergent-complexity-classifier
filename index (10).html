About the metrics

Lambda (λ) counts what fraction of rule outputs are alive. Rules near λ = 0.5 tend to produce complex behaviour.

Shannon Entropy measures how unpredictable each row is. High entropy means chaotic, low means ordered.

LZ Complexity counts how many distinct substrings are needed to build the output string from scratch. Low compression ratio means high complexity.
